About Us

Welcome to Tatt'up!
Born from a shared passion for the art of ink, we're not just another startup - we're a global community for tattoo enthusiasts.

How it works:

For tattooers and designers:

Tatt'up offers a platform to share your art with the world. Display your designs, cultivate a follower base and expand your horizons.

For Tattoo Enthusiasts:

Dive deep into an ocean of designs and styles. Whether you're looking for a delicate floral piece or a fierce dragon, our platform offers endless inspiration. And when you've found "the one"? Easily connect with the artist behind the masterpiece and ink your dreams into reality.

Monetize your Art

On our cutting-edge platform, passionate tattoo enthusiasts have the seamless ability to schedule appointments with you, simplifying the booking process while ensuring a secure and direct payment experience, all within the confines of our platform. We facilitate a smooth transaction by handling the payment process, allowing clients to compensate you effortlessly. In return for the convenience and efficiency our platform provides, we apply a nominal commission fee. This approach not only streamlines the entire appointment management cycle but also guarantees a hassle-free financial transaction for both you and your clients, marking a new era in the tattoo industry where convenience meets creativity.
